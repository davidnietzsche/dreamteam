# Elon Advisor Ingestion Policy

## Safe to Store in Git

- Source metadata
- URLs
- Dates
- Source types
- Short lawful summaries
- Topic tags
- Evaluation notes
- Prompt files
- Benchmarks and rubrics
- Short quoted snippets within fair-use-aware limits

## Store With Caution

- Public transcripts generated by third parties
- Video subtitles copied from platforms
- Long excerpts from books
- Paywalled articles

If rights or reuse terms are unclear, store only metadata and human-written summaries.

## Do Not Commit by Default

- Full copyrighted books
- Full audiobook text
- Full commercial transcripts
- Bulk copies of articles from paid publications
- Proprietary datasets you did not create

## Recommended Pattern

1. Keep the repo public and clean.
2. Keep a separate private ingestion workspace if you later obtain licensed material.
3. Convert everything into structured notes, tags, and evidence links before it reaches the public advisor layer.

## Advisor Integrity Rules

- Distinguish evidence from inference.
- Mark contradictory evidence explicitly.
- Prefer first-party source material when available.
- Never market outputs as if they were approved by Elon Musk.
