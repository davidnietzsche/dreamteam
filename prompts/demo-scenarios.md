# Demo Scenarios

Use these to prove the advisor is more than a style imitation.

1. Your EV company is burning cash and missing production targets. What would the Elon advisor cut first?
2. Your robotics team wants to add features before manufacturing is stable. What would the advisor say?
3. Your startup has five product lines and no dominant bottleneck. How would the advisor force prioritization?
4. Your leadership team wants consensus before every big move. How would the advisor redesign decision flow?
5. Your mission sounds inspiring, but unit economics are weak. How would the advisor reconcile mission with execution?
